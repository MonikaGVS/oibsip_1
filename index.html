AboutUs


The birth of the Breakfast Company can be traced back to 1999 when the first Breakfast opened its doors to its guests on a historical September day in Vienna.

Characterized by a very special and unimitable atmosphere, the Breakfast Company is a new, unique interpretation of the ever-popular traditional Viennese coffee house culuture, which was included in the UNESCO list of ,,intangible cultural heritage’’ in 2011.
